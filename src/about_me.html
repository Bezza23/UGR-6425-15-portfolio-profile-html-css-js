Let Me Tell You a Little About Myself

Background

Hello! I'm Bezawit Girma, a third-year software engineering student at the Addis Ababa Institute of Technology. I'm passionate about practical projects that challenge my skills. I grew up in Congo Sefer with 13 cousins, which was both chaotic and fun. Now, I live in Lege Tafo, just outside Addis Ababa. My values and personal growth were shaped by my time at Nazareth School, a girls-only Catholic institution, where I learned the importance of sisterhood, support, positivity, and self-respect.

What Are My Strongest Skills?

One of my strongest skills as a rising software developer is my attention to detail and problem-solving ability. I excel at breaking down complex problems into manageable parts and finding effective, creative solutions. As a perfectionist, I ensure that every detail is carefully examined, reflecting my commitment to excellence and continuous improvement. I maintain a positive outlook, viewing challenges as opportunities for growth, which keeps me motivated and resilient.
